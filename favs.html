FAVORITES

Slurping Turtle Tonkotsu ramen

The tonkotsu ramen from Slurping Turtle is a must try when in Ann Arbor. This creamy pork broth base soup with its amazing noodles and toppings will definitely not disappoint.

Slurping Turtle's Pork Belly Bao

Another one of our favorites is the Pork Belly Bao yet again from the delicious Slurping Turtle. The tender glazed pork belly is served in a pillowy bun as well as topped with a garnish. We highly recommend this appetizer to accompany your ramen.

No Thai Fried Rice

You cannot go wrong with No Thai's classic fried rice with white onion, green onion, peas, carrots, egg, with a savory sauce and your preferred spice level. This is one of our personal favorites after a long day of classes.

Sweetwater's Napali Brew

The Napali Brew is inspired by the sandy beaches of Hawaii's Napali Coast. It is made with vanilla ice cream, espresso & hazelnut syrup, topped with their housemade whipped cream. This taste of tropical paradise is the perfect treat after an exam or long week of studying.

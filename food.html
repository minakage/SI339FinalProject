FOOD

Zingerman's

One of Ann Arbor’s most prized possessions is Zingerman’s Delicatessen. Locals line up for generous deli sandwiches at this funky, longtime market with specialty groceries.

Frita Batidos

Voted Best Burger by Michigan Daily readers since 2014, Frita Batidos serves colorful cuban street food and tropical cocktails.

Miss Kim

Miss Kim is a trendy spot for traditional Korean fare with a modern, seasonal twist and contemporary ambiance.

Cafe Zola

Eclectic fare & global wines are served amid a rustic space with high ceilings & exposed brick. They draw inspiration from European, Mediterranean, and Turkish cuisine, bringing a global flair to Ann Arbor.

Mani Osteria

Located on E Liberty, Mani Osteria brings a taste of Italian fusion to UofM, with wood-oven pizza & Italian small plates accompanies with old-world wines & local beer.

Jerusalem Garden

This snug, comforting restaurant offers halal middle eastern cuisine and coffees. Family owned and authentic, Jerusalem Garden works hard to bring their heritage to life.

Sava's

This contemporary bi-level eatery serves eclectic, locally sourced fare & a large Sunday brunch buffet.

NYPD

Located on E. William, the New York Pizza Depot is a casual pizzeria known for its New York & Chicago style pies delivers classic chow late into the night. It’s known for its experimental, fruitful pizza toppings.

Tomukun

Tomukun is an upmarket noodle house and Korean BBQ restaurant that dishes up udon, ramen, & Pan-Asian mains amid a rich wood interior.

Madras Masala

Madras Masala has mastered the aristocracy of South Indian cooks and Indo-Chinese fusion. They boast an extensive menu of over 170 dishes & a lunch buffet.
